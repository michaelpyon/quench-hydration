Why SMS instead of an app?

Apps decay. You delete them, mute them, forget them. Texts cut through. Quench lives where you already pay attention, with zero install friction.

How do you calculate my goal?

We blend body weight, your wake window, and a climate signal. Then we space reminders so you're never chugging 32 oz before bed.

Will you spam me?

No. The nudge cadence is yours. You can SKIP any nudge or STOP the whole plan in one reply. We don't share your number.

Is it really free?

Yes during beta. We'll tell you well before that changes, and your plan keeps working either way.

Does it work outside the US?

Right now we're US-only while we tune the SMS infrastructure. International support is on the roadmap.
